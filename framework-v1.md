# FRAMEWORK: THE BRAND AUTHORITY INDEX v1.0

## 1. THE SOVEREIGNTY SCORE (SS)
`SS = (Entity_Density * 0.4) + (Citation_Velocity * 0.3) + (EEAT_Score * 0.3)`

*   **Entity_Density:** How well-defined your brand is in regional Knowledge Graphs.
*   **Citation_Velocity:** The rate at which AI models cite your proprietary data points.

## 2. THE 9 PRINCETON GEO METHODS
The BAI implements the Princeton GEO framework optimized for MENA:
1. **Statistics Addition:** High-impact data points.
2. **Citation Authority:** Claiming the source of truth.
3. **Answer-First Structure:** Machine-ingestible content.
4. **Linguistic Vectorization:** Dialect-aware semantic mapping.
