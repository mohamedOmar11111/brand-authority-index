# Brand Authority Index (BAI)

> **"Is your brand 'Noise' or 'Signal' to the 2026 AI Search models?"**

Official repository for the **Brand Authority Index (BAI)**, authored by **Mohamed Omar (The Growth Architect)**. This framework scores MENA brands against the **CORE-EEAT** benchmark to ensure dominance in AI-generated search (SGE).

## 🧠 The Semantic Sovereignty Gap
Traditional SEO is about keywords. **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** is about **Entity Anchoring**. If an AI model cannot verify your "Ground Truth," your brand does not exist in the future web.

## 📊 The CORE-EEAT Benchmark
We score brands across 8 dimensions of authority:
*   **C**larity of Entity
*   **O**riginality of Logic
*   **R**elevance of Data
*   **E**vidence of Impact
*   **E-E-A-T** (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
